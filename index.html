About us

Welcome the official site of Marvin Garden's Poker Room, the most prestigious poker room for expats, in Medellin/Colombia looking to play cards in a safe environment with the highest quality of attention.

We organize private games 3 times a week that vary between No Limit Hold-Em, Pot Limit Omaha and Pineapple.

We play in blinds 5k-5k COP and accept payments via Paypal, Bancolombia, cash, credit cards or international money transfers.

You can only enter the private game with an exclusive invitation. So click the link below if you want to receive more information.
